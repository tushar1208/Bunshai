Professional Biography

With over 15 years of experience in the technology industry, Mr. BunShai has established himself as a visionary leader and innovator in the digital transformation space. His journey began as a software developer, where he quickly recognized the potential of technology to transform businesses.

Under his leadership, BunShai TECHNOHUB has grown from a startup to a global technology solutions provider serving clients across multiple continents. His hands-on approach and deep technical expertise combined with strategic business acumen have been instrumental in the company's success.
